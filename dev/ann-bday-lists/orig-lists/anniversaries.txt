#   '11 may Kim & Damon {1999}'
# will be formatted as:
#   'Kim & Damon ann., 11'
#
# This entry
#   '11 may Kim & Damon [1999]'
# will be formatted as:
#   'Kim & Damon ann., 11th'
#
# Note any text after a bracketed year is ignored.
